Description

Stereo Boy is a sci-fi adventure featuring mind-bending puzzles, a unique teleportation mechanic, and striking, split-screen isometric environments.

You play as Blit, a plucky robot astronaut who's stranded on mysterious Planet SO-671. Something terrible happened to this place, and the world is split into two parallel dimensions. Luckily, Blit can jump between these dimensions, allowing you to traverse seemingly-impossible terrain. Navigate mazes, solves puzzles, blast the occasional spiky alien thing, and help Blit find a way home!

Features

  • Single-player, split-screen gameplay: SO-671's environments are divided into halves of the screen, representing parallel worlds. Using Blit's handy teleportation ability, you can zig-zag between these worlds, using bridges and ramps on one side to get to otherwise-unreachable areas in the other.
  • Solve puzzles across dimensions: Stereo Boy is chock-full of interlocking puzzle mechanics, requiring you to use Blit's teleportation ability in increasingly creative and intricate ways.
  • Light action, with a ray gun: No space adventure would be complete without hostile alien baddies. Players can confront them head-on, or avoid them with some clever cross-dimensional footwork.
  • Explore a lush, mysterious world: Stereo Boy is composed of 50+ striking, isometric levels. Players can collect shards to unlock bonus content, as well as discover dozens of logs depicting the tragic (and sometimes hilarious) last days of a lost civilization.
