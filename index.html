Meet BLIT.

You are an explorer from a distant world. Your mission: to investigate a newly discovered signal that could mean first contact with an alien world.

Something has gone wrong! Your expedition team is missing, and the planet you've arrived on is has split into multiple dimensions. Lucky for you, Blit's engine can harness the dimensional energy to jump between the parallel worlds. Will you be able to navigate the mysterious terrain, explore the planet's many secrets, and figure out what happened to this world?

50+ levels of mind-bending exploration and puzzling stand between you and getting home!
